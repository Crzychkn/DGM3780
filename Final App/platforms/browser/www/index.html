Frequently Asked Questions

What is Non Profit Co?

We are an organization created to help advance technology for the betterment of people everywhere. We help local communities and grassroots groups push policy that protects things like Net Neutrality.

How are you different?

We focus specifically on technology. There aren't many Non-profit organizations around that focus exclusively on technology and its potential to improve lives.

Do you hire?

You can check out our Join page to see how you can help contribute. If there is a great need for a specific role we do offer positions from time to time.

Why focus on technology?

We are passionate about all kinds of technology, so it is easier to push for. It's what we're good at!
